Job description

Teknobit Solutions is seeking a highly motivated and skilled Software Developer to join our growing team. In this role, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining high-quality software solutions that align with our clients’ needs and business goals. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to create innovative solutions, tackle complex challenges, and help drive the company’s technology vision forward.

Responsibility

  • Develop, test, and deploy software applications and systems as per project requirements.
  • Work closely with clients and internal teams to understand business requirements and provide technical solutions.
  • Collaborate with developers, designers, and other team members to ensure seamless integration and implementation of systems.
  • Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues, providing timely support and assistance.
  • Maintain and update software, ensuring security standards and best practices are followed.
  • Contribute to project planning, documentation, and post-deployment support.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends, technologies, and best practices.
  • Provide mentorship to junior team members and assist in their professional growth.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • Proven experience in [Specific Technology/Tools] (e.g., Java, Python, .NET, etc.).
  • Strong knowledge of software development methodologies and best practices.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills with the ability to analyze complex technical issues
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills for effective collaboration.
  • Familiarity with Agile development processes.
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, etc.) is a plus.
